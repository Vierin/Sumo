Cloud Software Program

The technology for SUMO consists of a cloud-native stack to support the high capacity and high-velocity data that continues to grow. Cloud storage has proven to be better for big data companies and industries versus traditional desktop storage and systems.

T³ Platform

With the needs of different industries varying wildly, SUMO was created with a T³ platform so that users can tailor the platform to the data analytics they need to track and make decisions based on. The platform completes the heavy lifting so that calculating outcomes and treatment planning are noticeably enhanced.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) has immensely grown where many functions in healthcare can be automated. Health risk assessments known as HRAs can be automated in SUMO to improve patient evaluations for better treatment. Other AI functions can include user support and other functions tailored to your needs.

Clinical Graph Structure

The clinical graph structure in SUMO promotes improved visibility that can help see data in a way not previously available. The connection of data from various sources such as clinics, wearable technology, medical professionals, and more for each patient creates a more thorough explanation of their medical background. This can result in better treatment, health plan selections, and proactive diagnoses.

Expertise Support

The SUMO team consists of senior doctors and professors with specialisations in musculoskeletal and orthopedic care. They can provide the support needed to make SUMO a successful tool for you.

Data Outcomes

With the unique approach for each user of SUMO, data outcomes are tailored to the analytics you need. Specific datasets can be chosen when running data analytics. The versatility of SUMO allows for various integrations. With ways to monitor health such as wearable technology, data can go directly to the SUMO software and be used in health analysis for the patient using it.
