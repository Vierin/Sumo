How it Works

A few confident steps towards automation

  • Step 1

    Encrypted data is automatically uploaded onto our secure system.

  • Step 2

    Nodes of specific data are automatically created (for example patient information, wait times, procedure details, recovery processes, treatment costs and so on).

  • Step 3

    The data is automatically analysed by our leading software, and relevant links are made between the information. Each result is specific to your pre-determined requirements and key to solve your particular task.

  • Step 4

    SUMO provide you and your company with the tools required to operate the software independently. We equip you with information to keep track of patient history and their treatment process, to control their recovery programme and improve your general service by reducing wait times or limiting paperwork for example.
